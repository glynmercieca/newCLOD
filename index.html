This site presents two datasets. One has data about Local Councils of Malta, gathered from lc.gov.mt in late 2013. It has information on mayors and the other councillors along with contact details. The other dataset contains information about entertainment venues in Malta gathered from visitmalta.com and tripadvisor.com. Information about each venue, contact details and amenities is present.
